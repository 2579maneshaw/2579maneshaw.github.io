Friends & Family Booking Request

Use this private form to request a friends and family rate. The daily rate will be set by the pricing model used to stay competitive, but removes the Property Management's booking fee. We’ll confirm availability and pricing by text or email.

Before you submit this request
Please click here to check current pricing and availability on Airbnb for your desired dates.
  • The price shown on Airbnb includes Airbnb and property management booking fees.
  • If approved as Friends/Family, the property management booking fee will be removed. Cleaning fee and all other fees still apply.
  • All requests from this page must be verified and approved by the owner.
  • A stay is not considered booked until payment is received by the property manager.
  • Submitting this form is a request only, not a confirmed reservation.
