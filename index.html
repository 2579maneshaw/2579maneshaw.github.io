Enchanted Villa of Windsor Hills

A calm, family-friendly Disney retreat where the magic doesn’t end when the parks close.

Disney is about wonder and excitement. The Enchanted Villa of Windsor Hills is about the pause that makes the magic last.

After full days of rides, parades, and nonstop energy, our villa offers families a serene, spa-inspired retreat where every evening ends in calm, comfort, and connection.

While kids enjoy Disney-inspired touches and access to Windsor Hills’ incredible resort amenities, parents can relax in quiet, beautifully appointed spaces or unwind in the private splash pool under the stars.

  • Private screened-in splash pool with year-round pool heat included
  • Spa-inspired interiors designed for rest and relaxation
  • Multiple ensuite bedrooms for comfort and privacy
  • Walkable access to Windsor Hills clubhouse and waterpark
  • Minutes from Disney parks and attractions
